ArtYard Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2025

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, profile photo, nickname)
  • Location data (optional, for artwork tagging)
  • Messages and artworks you share
  • App usage data (likes, bookmarks)

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our service
  • To enable communication between users
  • To prevent spam and maintain community safety
  • To provide customer support

Data Protection

  • All data is encrypted and securely stored
  • We do not share personal information with third parties
  • Users can request data deletion at any time
  • Location sharing is optional and can be disabled

Your Rights

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of location data collection
  • Delete your account and all associated data

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@artyard.app

Or through our app support system.

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
