Artist Guidelines

Last updated: November 21, 2024

Welcome to Artyard! These guidelines ensure a fair, safe, and creative community for all artists and collectors. By selling on Artyard, you agree to follow these rules.

Artwork Listing Requirements

Authenticity & Originality

✅ DO

  • Upload only YOUR original work
  • Create all elements yourself
  • Disclose AI assistance if used
  • Own full rights to the work

❌ DON'T

  • Copy other artists' work
  • Use copyrighted images
  • Claim AI art as handmade
  • Steal or plagiarize

Secondary Market & Resale Policy

Building a Healthy Art Market Together

Artyard supports a vibrant secondary market that benefits both artists and collectors. Reselling is allowed to create market liquidity and value discovery.

✅ Resale is Allowed:

  • Collectors: You may freely resell artworks you own
  • Galleries/Dealers: Professional resellers are welcome
  • Market Liquidity: Active secondary market increases artwork value
  • Transparency: Honest disclosure of resale status is encouraged

❌ Prohibited Resale Practices:

  • Fraudulent Listings: Selling counterfeit or unauthorized copies
  • Price Manipulation: Coordinated fake transactions to inflate prices
  • Self-Dealing: Buying from yourself to manipulate market
  • Stolen Artwork: Reselling work without proof of ownership
  • Misrepresentation: False claims about artwork provenance or authenticity

Resale Best Practices

  • Accurate Description: Provide honest condition report
  • Artist Attribution: Always credit original artist correctly
  • Clear Photos: Show current condition of artwork
  • Fair Pricing: Price based on current market value
  • Respectful Communication: Maintain professional standards

Photo Quality Standards

High-quality photos sell better!

  • Resolution: Minimum 1000x1000 pixels (1500x1500 recommended)
  • Lighting: Well-lit, natural or professional lighting
  • Focus: Sharp, clear images showing texture and detail
  • Angles: Multiple views for 3D work and sculptures
  • Context: Show scale with room shots or measurements
  • Color Accuracy: Photos should represent true colors

Prohibited in Photos:

  • Blurry or pixelated images
  • Heavy filters that distort colors
  • Watermarks covering the artwork
  • Photos of screens (re-photograph originals)

Accurate Descriptions

✅ Be Honest

  • True dimensions and materials
  • Disclose all imperfections
  • Clear about what's included
  • Honest about artwork condition

❌ Don't Mislead

  • Exaggerate size or quality
  • Hide damage or flaws
  • Use deceptive pricing
  • Fake scarcity tactics

Prohibited Content

The following content will result in immediate removal and possible account suspension:

Strictly Prohibited

  • Illegal Content: Anything violating local or international law
  • Child Exploitation: Any content involving minors in inappropriate ways
  • Violence/Gore: Extremely graphic violent imagery
  • Hate Symbols: Nazi imagery, hate group symbols, discriminatory content
  • Pornography: Explicit sexual content (artistic nudity may be allowed with proper tagging)

Requires Careful Handling

  • Artistic Nudity: Tag appropriately, must be tasteful and artistic
  • Political Art: Allowed but must not incite violence or hatred
  • Religious Content: Respectful treatment of all religions
  • Controversial Topics: Present thoughtfully and respectfully

Transaction Conduct

Communication Standards

Professional communication builds trust and reputation

  • Response Time: Reply to buyer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Professional Tone: Courteous, helpful, and clear
  • Transparency: Be upfront about shipping times and policies
  • Problem-Solving: Work with buyers to resolve issues

Prohibited Communication:

  • Harassment, threats, or abuse
  • Asking for payment outside platform
  • Sharing personal contact info prematurely
  • Spam or unsolicited advertising

Shipping Excellence

  • Ship Within 5 Days: From payment confirmation
  • Pack Professionally: Prevent damage with proper materials
  • Provide Tracking: Upload within 24 hours of shipment
  • Communicate Delays: Notify buyers immediately of any issues
  • Personal Touch: Include thank-you note or certificate

Pricing Ethics

✅ Fair Pricing

  • Realistic market value
  • Consistent across platforms
  • Clear about fees and shipping
  • Transparent pricing structure

❌ Price Manipulation

  • Artificial price inflation
  • Fake auction bidding
  • Bait-and-switch tactics
  • Hidden costs after purchase

Violation Consequences

Warning System

First Violation (Minor):

  • Official warning notification
  • 7-day listing suspension
  • Required policy review

Second Violation:

  • 30-day account suspension
  • All listings removed
  • Payout delay during review
  • Potential permanent restrictions

Third Violation or Serious Offense:

  • Permanent account termination
  • Payment hold (up to 90 days for existing orders)
  • Removal from all challenges and auctions
  • No appeal for serious violations

Immediate Ban Offenses

The following result in immediate permanent ban:

  • Fraud or scam attempts
  • Selling stolen artwork
  • Harassment or threats
  • Illegal content
  • Repeated copyright infringement
  • Fake reviews or rating manipulation

Best Practices for Success

Build a Strong Profile

  • Complete Bio: Tell your artistic story (150-300 words)
  • Professional Photo: Clear headshot or studio photo
  • Social Links: Connect Instagram, website, etc.
  • Regular Updates: Keep profile current with latest work
  • Artist Statement: Share your inspiration and process

Maximize Visibility

Active artists get more exposure!

  • Post Regularly: 1-2 new artworks per week minimum
  • Join Challenges: Participate in bi-weekly challenges
  • Engage Community: Comment, like, and support others
  • Use Tags Wisely: Relevant, specific keywords
  • Share Process: Behind-the-scenes content
  • Respond Quickly: Fast replies boost your ranking

Pricing Strategy

Smart Pricing Tips:

  • Start Competitive: Research similar artists' pricing
  • Build Gradually: Raise prices as your following grows
  • Edition Sizes: Limited editions command higher prices
  • Range of Prices: Offer both affordable and premium pieces
  • Factor Everything: Materials + time + overhead + profit

Portfolio Curation

  • Quality Over Quantity: Show only your best 20-50 pieces
  • Cohesive Style: Diverse but recognizable aesthetic
  • Lead with Best: First 6-9 artworks are most critical
  • Update Regularly: Remove weaker pieces, add new work
  • Variety: Mix of sizes, styles, and price points

Shipping Excellence

  • Fast Turnaround: Ship same or next business day if possible
  • Quality Materials: Invest in proper packaging
  • Include Card: Business card or artist info
  • Personal Touch: Handwritten thank-you note
  • Branded Packaging: Memorable unboxing experience

Build Reputation

Your reputation is your most valuable asset

  • Maintain 4.5+ Rating: Consistently excellent service
  • Resolve Issues Fast: Turn problems into positive experiences
  • Professional Always: Even with difficult buyers
  • Exceed Expectations: Under-promise, over-deliver
  • Request Reviews: Politely ask satisfied buyers

Tips for Growth

Social Media Integration

  • Cross-post to Instagram, Pinterest, Twitter
  • Use consistent username across platforms
  • Share Artyard profile link in bio
  • Show behind-the-scenes content
  • Engage with art community hashtags

Challenge Participation

  • Submit to every relevant challenge
  • Follow theme guidelines carefully
  • Vote on other entries (build goodwill)
  • Auction wins boost credibility
  • Learn from winning entries

Customer Service Excellence

  • Answer questions before they're asked (detailed descriptions)
  • Offer multiple photos from different angles
  • Provide size comparisons or room mockups
  • Clear policies on returns and customs
  • Go above and beyond for VIP buyers
