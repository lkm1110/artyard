# Privacy Policy for ArtYard

**Last Updated: January 2025**

---

## Introduction

ArtYard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use ArtYard, we collect:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, username (handle), password
- **Profile Information**: Profile picture, bio, location (optional)
- **Artist Information**: Portfolio, artwork details, pricing information
- **Payment Information**: Payment card details (processed securely through our payment processor)
- **Communication Data**: Messages you send to other users, support inquiries

### 2. Artwork Information

- **Images**: Photos of artworks you upload
- **Metadata**: Title, description, dimensions, materials, year created, price
- **Location Data**: Optional location where artwork was created (if you choose to share)

### 3. Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features you use, pages you visit, time spent on the app
- **Location Data**: General location (city/country) if you grant permission
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, access times, crash reports

### 4. Camera and Photo Library

- We access your camera and photo library only when you choose to upload artwork images
- We do not access or store any photos other than those you explicitly select to upload

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### Primary Purposes:
- **Provide Services**: Create and manage your account, display artwork, facilitate transactions
- **Communication**: Connect artists and collectors, send notifications about sales, messages, and updates
- **Payment Processing**: Process artwork purchases and artist payouts
- **Improve Services**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, protect against security threats
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

### Optional Features:
- **Personalization**: Recommend artwork based on your interests (if you opt-in)
- **Marketing**: Send promotional content about new features (you can opt-out anytime)
- **Analytics**: Understand how users interact with our app (anonymized data)

## Data Tracking and Advertising

### User Tracking:

ArtYard includes **NSUserTrackingUsageDescription** for optional tracking features:

- **Purpose**: To deliver personalized artwork recommendations and relevant content
- **Your Choice**: We will ask for your permission before tracking
- **Opt-Out**: You can decline or change your preference anytime in Settings
- **What We Track**: If you consent, we may track your interactions within the app to improve recommendations

**We do NOT track you for third-party advertising purposes.**

### Analytics:

We use analytics to understand:
- How users navigate the app
- Which features are most popular
- Performance issues and crashes
- This data is **anonymized** and does not identify you personally

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- **Secure Storage**: Passwords are hashed and salted
- **Access Control**: Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Payment Security**: PCI-DSS compliant payment processing

**However**, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Most data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Obligations**: Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records)
- **Artwork**: If you delete your account, your artwork listings are permanently removed
