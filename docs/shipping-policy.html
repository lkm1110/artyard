Shipping Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2024

Artyard connects artists and collectors worldwide. Each seller is responsible for shipping their artworks safely and promptly. This policy outlines shipping responsibilities and expectations.

Seller Responsibilities

As a seller, you must:

  • Pack Securely: Use appropriate materials to prevent damage
    • Bubble wrap or foam for protection
    • Rigid cardboard or wooden crate for large pieces
    • Waterproof outer packaging
    • Clear "FRAGILE" and "THIS SIDE UP" labels
  • Ship Promptly: Ship within 5 business days of payment confirmation
  • Provide Tracking: Upload tracking number within 24 hours of shipment
  • Insurance: Strongly recommended for items over $500
  • Communication: Keep buyer informed of any delays

Shipping Timeframes

Shipping Type Estimated Delivery
Domestic (Same Country) 3-7 business days
International (Express) 7-14 business days
International (Standard) 14-21 business days

Note: Customs clearance and local delivery may add additional time for international shipments.

Shipping Costs

⚠️ Important: Seller Responsibility

The seller is responsible for all shipping costs and arrangements. Artyard does not handle shipping directly.

  • Seller Responsibility: Sellers bear all shipping costs and must arrange shipment themselves
  • Direct Communication: Buyers and sellers can discuss shipping details through in-app chat
  • Sold Out Management: Sellers must mark items as "Sold Out" after completing a sale (including direct deals)
  • Flexible Options: Sellers may offer local pickup or direct delivery through chat negotiations

Shipping Cost Guidelines

  • Small prints (under 12"): $10-20 domestic, $20-40 international
  • Medium artworks (12"-24"): $20-50 domestic, $50-100 international
  • Large artworks (24"+): $50-150 domestic, $150-300 international
  • Sculptures: Varies by weight and fragility

Lost or Damaged Packages

Direct Transaction

Buyer and seller resolve directly

Artyard is not responsible for shipping issues in direct transactions. We recommend using escrow for valuable items.

Escrow Transaction

Protected by Artyard Buyer Protection

  • Report Immediately: Within 48 hours of delivery for damage
  • Provide Evidence: Photos of packaging and damage
  • Investigation: Admin reviews case within 3-5 business days
  • Resolution: Refund or replacement if seller at fault

Tracking & Updates

  • Sellers: Must provide tracking number within 24 hours of shipment
  • Buyers: Can check shipping status in "My Orders"
  • Notifications: Automatic updates when status changes
  • Delays: Sellers must notify buyers of any shipping delays

International Shipping

Important for International Buyers:

  • Customs Fees: Buyer is responsible for all customs duties and taxes
  • Import Regulations: Check your country's art import rules
  • Customs Delays: Can add 5-14 days to delivery time
  • Documentation: Seller must provide customs declaration
  • Prohibited Items: Some countries restrict certain materials or sizes

Customs Value Declaration

Sellers must declare the actual sale price. Under-declaring customs value is illegal and can result in:

  • Package seizure
  • Fines or penalties
  • Account suspension

Packaging Best Practices

For Paintings & Prints

  1. Cover artwork face with glassine or acid-free paper
  2. Place cardboard on both sides
  3. Wrap in bubble wrap (3-4 layers)
  4. Place in rigid cardboard mailer or box
  5. Fill empty space with packing material

For Framed Works

  1. Tape an "X" across glass to prevent shattering
  2. Protect corners with cardboard corner guards
  3. Wrap entire frame in bubble wrap
  4. Use double-walled box
  5. Add "FRAGILE - GLASS" labels

For Sculptures

  1. Wrap each part separately if disassemblable
  2. Use foam peanuts or bubble wrap generously
  3. Sturdy wooden crate for valuable pieces
  4. Include assembly instructions if needed

Delivery Confirmation

  • Signature Required: Recommended for items over $500
  • Photo Proof: Delivery services may provide photo confirmation
  • Mark as Received: Buyers should confirm receipt in app
  • Inspect Immediately: Check for damage upon delivery

Buyer Protection Period

For Escrow Transactions:

  • 7 days from delivery to open dispute
  • Report damage within 48 hours
  • Keep all packaging for potential returns
  • Take photos of packaging and artwork immediately

Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to seller:

  • Seller must notify buyer immediately
  • Buyer can request re-shipment (pays new shipping)
  • Or buyer can request refund (minus original shipping)
  • Seller not responsible if buyer provided wrong address
