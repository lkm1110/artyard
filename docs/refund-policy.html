Refund Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2024

At Artyard, we strive to ensure both buyers and sellers have a fair and transparent transaction experience. Our refund policy varies depending on the transaction type.

Direct Transaction (0% fee)

No platform refunds available

For direct transactions, buyers and sellers must negotiate directly. Artyard is not responsible for disputes or refunds in direct transactions.

  • All sales are final through the platform
  • Buyer and seller communicate directly for any issues
  • Platform provides messaging but no refund processing
  • We recommend using escrow for valuable items

Escrow Transaction (10% fee)

Protected by Artyard Buyer Protection

Escrow transactions include platform protection and refund processing.

Eligible Refund Reasons

  • Item Significantly Different: Artwork does not match description or photos
  • Damaged During Shipping: Item arrived damaged (must report within 48 hours)
  • Item Not Received: Package lost or never delivered
  • Authenticity Issues: Item is not original as described

Refund Request Process

  1. Open Dispute: Must be within 7 days of delivery confirmation
  2. Provide Evidence: Upload clear photos showing the issue
  3. Admin Review: Our team reviews within 3-5 business days
  4. Decision: If approved, full refund including platform fees

Return Shipping

  • Seller's Fault: Seller pays return shipping
  • Buyer's Fault: Buyer pays return shipping
  • Original artwork must be returned in same condition
  • Use insured shipping for valuable items

Non-Refundable Cases

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

  • Buyer changed their mind after purchase
  • Item accurately matches description and photos
  • Minor color differences due to screen calibration
  • Buyer's remorse or preference changes
  • After 7-day dispute window has passed
  • Buyer damaged the item after delivery

Challenge & Auction Items

All sales are final for auction wins

  • No refunds for challenge auction items
  • Review artwork details carefully before bidding
  • Bids are binding commitments
  • Contact seller directly for any pre-bid questions

Refund Timeline

  • Dispute Review: 3-5 business days
  • Refund Processing: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days

Total time: 11-20 business days from dispute resolution
