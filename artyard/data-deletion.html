ArtYard Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At ArtYard, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to delete your data, what happens when you delete your account, and what data may be retained.

1. Your Right to Delete Data

You have the right to delete your ArtYard account and associated data at any time, for any reason. The deletion process is:

  • Simple - Can be done directly in the app in just a few taps
  • Immediate - Most data is deleted right away
  • Permanent - Deleted data cannot be recovered
  • Free - No fees or charges for account deletion

4. What Happens After Deletion

4.1 Immediate Effects

  • Your profile disappears from search results
  • Your artworks are removed from the marketplace
  • Other users cannot see your profile or artworks
  • You are logged out of all devices
  • Your email address becomes available for new registrations after 30 days

4.2 Cannot Be Recovered

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered. You will need to:
  • Create a new account if you want to use ArtYard again
  • Re-upload all your artworks
  • Rebuild your followers and connections

4.3 Impact on Other Users

  • Purchases: If someone bought your artwork, they keep access to it (they paid for it)
  • Comments: Your comments on other users' artworks are deleted
  • Messages: Conversations with you are deleted from both sides
  • Followers: Your followers list is deleted; they lose you from their following list

6. Data Retention for Legal Compliance

Some data may be retained for legal or security reasons:

6.1 Financial Records (7 Years)

If you sold artworks on ArtYard, we are legally required to keep transaction records for tax purposes. These records are:

  • Anonymized - Your name and email are removed
  • Secured - Stored in encrypted format
  • Not Shared - Only accessible to tax authorities if legally required
  • Auto-Deleted - Permanently deleted after 7 years

6.2 Security Logs (90 Days)

For security and fraud prevention, we keep:

  • Login IP addresses (anonymized after 90 days)
  • Reported content or accounts you reported
  • If your account was banned, ban records (to prevent re-registration)

7. Special Situations

7.1 Legal Hold

If your account is involved in legal proceedings, we may be required to preserve data until the matter is resolved.

7.2 Account Suspension vs. Deletion

  • Suspended Account - You can still delete it; data deletion follows the same process
  • Banned Account - You can request deletion via email; ban records are kept to prevent re-registration

7.3 Deceased User's Account

Family members can request account deletion by contacting us at privacy@artyard.app with proof of death certificate and relationship to the deceased.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR / CCPA

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights:

European Union (GDPR)

  • Right to be Forgotten - Request complete data deletion
  • Right to Access - Download all your data
  • Right to Rectification - Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Data Portability - Export your data

California (CCPA)

  • Right to Delete - Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Know - Request disclosure of data collected
  • Right to Opt-Out - Opt-out of data selling (we don't sell data!)
